About

Every moment holds a story worth telling beautifully.

I am Larissa Mendy, a filmmaker and visual storyteller with a deep passion for capturing the authentic emotion behind every scene. Through the lens, I seek the light that makes ordinary moments extraordinary — the glance between two people, the quiet joy in a room full of celebration.

Working across weddings, events, and photography, I bring a cinematic sensibility and an attentive eye to every project. My work is defined by the belief that beauty lies in the details, and that the best stories are those felt as deeply as they are seen.

The symbol phi — the golden ratio — guides everything I do. It is nature's own measure of beauty and harmony, present in the spiral of a shell, the arrangement of petals, the perfect frame of a face.

In every film and photograph, I search for that same proportion — the balance between light and shadow, motion and stillness, emotion and restraint. This is what The Symbol φ Studio is built on.
